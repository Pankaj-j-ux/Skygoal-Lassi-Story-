About Us

It is a company emerged out of people’s request. We left our International careers and following our dream to create a platform where your entrepreneurs can flourish and achieve their goals. We started our journey with QSR group, especially with Lassi in Dec 2017 and have experience of opening 50 outlets in Hyderabad alone in span of 12 months. With this mission, we want to put franchisee in the focus.

Therefore, we decided to start Lassi n Shakes to create possibilities to new entrepreneurs with start up support, Service, training, Quality maintenance and decent profits.

With Lassi n shakes offering we want to give much better taste and better service to both our franchisees and to end customers.
